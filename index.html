Introducing the TaleSpinner App - the newest launch product from Pupal Stage Publications. TaleSpinner engages children to read stories by putting them into the story. They aren't just reading about a trip to the beach, they are reading about their trip to the beach.

Pupal Stage Publications helps parents, teachers, and caregivers create an engaging experience for their children through customized book creation. Our application and publications will help customers produce tangible books – Lexile leveled books – specially made for each child’s unique world. While the books we create are naturally readable on smartphones, tablets, and other electronics – we are also excited to enable paperback deliveries to your front door in just a few days.
